## 📝 How to Customize

### Quick Customization (5 minutes)
1. Open `src/components/Portfolio.jsx`
2. Find and replace all instances of:
   - `PLACEHOLDER` → Your name
   - `placeholder@email.com` → Your email
   - Update social media URLs
3. Save and refresh

### Timeline Customization (10 minutes)
In `Portfolio.jsx`, locate the `timelineEvents` array and:
- Add your own dates
- Write your story
- Link to your projects/companies
- Remove or add entries as needed

### Color Customization (2 minutes)
In `tailwind.config.js`, change:
```javascript
'custom': '#fb6e1c'  // Your brand color
```
